About

Hi! My name is Cadence.

I'm a soundtrack composer, music producer, vocal performer, and Foley artist. I'm also working on a few game projects as a composer, sound designer, writer, programmer, and 2D visual artist — some alone, some as part of a team.

Other Music

I also write some experimental non-soundtrack music. Exterior Dangers is an experimental electronic pop album about my experience as a queer person.

Cooking Caregiver is a(n imaginary) video game about cooking (DIY) hormone replacement therapy. The game’s title is play on the video game franchise Cooking Mama, and the gameplay is implied to parody its genre. Unlike the “game,” the OST is real; it was written for the OST Composing Jam #6. Trans rights are human rights.
